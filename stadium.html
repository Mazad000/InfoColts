Lucas Oil Stadium

Home of the Indianapolis Colts — 500 South Capitol Avenue, Indianapolis, IN.

Quick facts

  • Capacity: ~63,000–67,000 (football configuration)
  • Address: 500 South Capitol Ave, Indianapolis, IN
  • Features: Retractable roof, massive HD screens, executive suites, modern amenities
  • Notable: Excellent downtown location with guided tours available

Plan your visit

Ticketing, parking, and accessibility info are available on the Colts' official site. Expect a mix of indoor comfort and iconic downtown views — the stadium's large moving window gives a great skyline sightline.
